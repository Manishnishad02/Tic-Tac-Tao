🎮 Play Tic-Tac-Toe

🎮 How to Play

  • 👤 Two players take turns to place X or O in an empty cell.
  • ✔ The first player to get three of their marks in a row (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) wins.
  • 🔄 If the board fills up without a winner, the game is a draw.
  • 🔘 Click the Reset button to start a new round.
